The Murder of Colin Roach


Colin Roach was murdered at 6:09 in the morning. At the crime scene, the DNA of several people were found. The people in question were:

  • Taing Seav Yong
  • Phou Jipo
  • Hanna Chai Yanyan

Each person involved have skillfully evaded the authorities. It is assumed that they used the Death Wand and anti-Colin Roach spray to kill him. The events of his death have been listed below:

  • Colin Roach is chased by Yong.
  • Hanna attempts to Death Wand him.
  • Colin manages to evade them, until Jipo manages to catch him.
  • Colin is restrained by Yong, and sprayed by anti-Colin Roach spray.
  • Colin Roach dies.
